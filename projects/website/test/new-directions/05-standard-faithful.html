4 section 04 / 04 — studio
brooklyn 2010 → seattle 2026
a small studio
in a small room.

social print studio is ten people. it was founded in 2010 in a brooklyn apartment by ben lotan and a small group of friends who wanted to print their own photos and could not find a single online lab they liked. today the studio is in seattle. most of the actual printing happens at long-running partner labs across the united states; the studio designs the products, writes the software, picks the paper, and answers the phone.

we are not a venture-backed marketplace. we are not an app. we are a printing house with a small website and a long memory. fifteen birthdays, sixteen years, eight products, ten people. that is the whole company on a single line.
